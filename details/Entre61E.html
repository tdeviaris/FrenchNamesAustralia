Section of the west part of the Archipelago of La Recherche, from the map ‘Carte de l'archipel de la Recherche, situé à la partie occidentale de la terre de Nuyts, reconnu par le contre-amiral Bruny-Dentrecasteaux, en décembre 1792 (an 1er de l'ère Française) ‘, by Beautemps-Beaupré. In Atlas du voyage de Bruny-Dentrecasteaux, 1807. Op. cit., plate14. Source: National Library of Australia https://nla.gov.au/nla.obj-230811677/view

This section of Beautemps-Beaupré’s chart of the archipelago depicts the route of the frigates upon their arrival on December 9, 1792 (1rst year in the French Revolutionary calendar). As night fell and a storm approached, the expedition found itself in a maze of islands, islets, and reefs, before finally seeking refuge on the northeast coast of Observatory Island.
