According to some DNA testing and historical research, Kaspar Hauser, a German youth said to have grown up in isolation in a darkened cell, may have been Stephanie’s son, but the proof of this affiliation needs more inquiries.

Further information: Baudin named the head Pointe Pitoresque (meaning picturesque), probably because of the aesthetic and scenic appearance of the Witton Bluff.
