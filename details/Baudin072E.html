Letter 34 taken from Lettres d'une Péruvienne written by Graffigny, F.. English translation: 1805. Peruvian Letters: Including the Letters of Aza, Volumes 1 à 2 London: T. Jones. https://archive.org/details/letterswrittenby00graf In this 34th letter, Mme de Graffigny criticizes the French superficial education that female received, based on appearance, with no real insight and understanding.

Freycinet reported that “below this last point [Pointe Graffigny], the shore [now known as Macs Beach] recedes into a shallow bay, approximately 10 miles wide.” (Au-dessous de ce dernier point, le rivage se renfonce en une baie peu profonde, de 10 milles d'ouverture environ ).
