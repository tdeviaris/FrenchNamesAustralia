Caffarelli, etching by Dutertre, A. between 1799-1801 Portrait from the set of portraits drawn and engraved by André Dutertre, that represent members of the Egyptian Expedition (1798-1801). Dutertre's portraits were published in ‘Histoire scientifique et militaire de l'expédition française en égypte’, in 10 volumes published for Louis Reybaud, Achille de Vaulabelle and Jean-Joseph Marcel, in 1830-1836.
