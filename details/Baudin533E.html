Île Caffarelli - Caffarelli Island

In this letter, Louis Marie Joseph Maximilien François Auguste Caffarelli du Falga, then serving as maritime prefect, requests authorisation from the Minister of War to obtain a copy of a sketch of the port of Brest held at the Dépôt des cartes in Paris. Executed with precision by engineers, this document seemed particularly useful to him for the defence of Brest and the conduct of naval operations. He explains that he intended to have a new chart of the port and its approaches prepared, and that access to this plan would save him valuable time in the context of the Breton coast, which was often affected by fog and poor visibility.

For Louis Marie Maximilien de Caffarelli du Falga, see Cap Caffarelli (South Australia).
